San Francisco is famous for its Golden Gate Bridge, steep streets, Alcatraz, and – you got it, dude! – Full House. It is a cultural and financial centre of the western United States and one of the country's most cosmopolitan cities. Locals appreciate nature, sustainable food, and art. Statistically speaking, San Francisco is safer than New York City. According to FBI data, in 2019, there were 5,933 violent crimes and 48,780 property crimes committed in San Francisco, while New York City had 47,821 and 122,299, respectively.
